
# Botium Toys: Cybersecurity Audit and Risk Assessment

## Project Description
This project focuses on a comprehensive cybersecurity audit for **Botium Toys**, a fictional retailer. [cite_start]The audit covers the organization’s entire security program, including all physical and digital assets, internal processes, and procedures[cite: 102, 103]. [cite_start]The primary objective is to evaluate the current security posture, identify vulnerabilities, and determine which controls and compliance best practices must be implemented to reduce risk[cite: 104, 118].

As part of the **Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate**, this lab involves:
* **Defining the Audit Scope**: Assessing on-premises equipment, employee devices, and legacy systems.
* **Risk Assessment**: Identifying inadequate asset management and evaluating risks to critical data.
* **Gap Analysis**: Comparing current security measures against industry standards like the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

---

## Experience & Skills Gained

### 1. Risk Assessment & Management
* Calculated a **Risk Score** (rated 8/10) based on current security gaps and potential business impact.
* Analyzed the impact of losing assets on **business continuity**.
* Identified critical vulnerabilities, such as a lack of **disaster recovery plans** and data **backups**.

### 2. Control Categorization & Evaluation
* Evaluated and categorized controls into **Administrative**, **Technical**, and **Physical** groups.
* Identified and assigned **Control Types**, including:
    * **Preventative**: Implementing least privilege and password policies.
    * **Detective**: Utilizing CCTV and Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS).
    * **Corrective**: Establishing backups and disaster recovery to restore assets.
    * **Deterrent**: Using signage and locks to discourage unauthorized access.

### 3. Compliance Auditing
* **PCI DSS**: Audited the handling of credit card information and identified the need for data encryption at transaction touchpoints.
* **GDPR**: Verified the existence of a 72-hour breach notification plan for E.U. customers and enforced privacy policies.
* **SOC (Type 1 & 2)**: Assessed user access policies and data integrity to ensure sensitive information remains confidential.

### 4. Strategic Security Recommendations
* Recommended the implementation of **least privilege** and **separation of duties** to limit unauthorized internal access.
* Advised on the adoption of a **centralized password management system** to enforce minimum complexity requirements.
* Prioritized the installation of an **IDS** to monitor for anomalous network traffic.
